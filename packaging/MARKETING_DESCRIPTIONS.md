## App Store Description (Full)

### English

**Say goodbye to copy-paste formatting nightmares.**

XtoMarkdown is the fastest way to convert your documents to Markdown. Whether you're a developer documenting code, a writer publishing to the web, or anyone who needs clean, portable text - XtoMarkdown has you covered.

**Simply drag and drop** your files onto the window and watch them transform into beautifully formatted Markdown. No complicated menus, no confusing options, no subscription fees.

**Supported Formats:**
• Microsoft Word (.docx)
• PDF documents (.pdf)
• PowerPoint presentations (.pptx)
• Excel spreadsheets (.xlsx)
• HTML files (.html)
• Rich Text Format (.rtf)
• OpenDocument formats (.odt, .odp, .ods)
• EPUB ebooks (.epub)
• CSV data files (.csv)
• JSON and XML files

**Key Features:**
• Drag-and-drop interface - just drop files and go
• Batch conversion - convert multiple files at once
• Two powerful conversion engines - choose the best tool for each format
• Preserves structure - headings, lists, tables, and more
• Clean output - no messy artifacts or broken formatting
• Works offline - your documents never leave your computer
• Cross-platform - same great experience on Windows, Mac, and Linux

**Perfect For:**
• Developers writing documentation
• Technical writers and content creators
• Bloggers migrating content
• Students converting notes
• Anyone moving to Markdown-based workflows

**Why Markdown?**
Markdown is the universal format for text. It works everywhere - GitHub, Notion, Obsidian, Jekyll, Hugo, and thousands of other tools. Once your content is in Markdown, you're free from vendor lock-in forever.

**Privacy First**
XtoMarkdown works entirely on your computer. Your documents are never uploaded anywhere. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

Download XtoMarkdown today and take control of your documents.
