Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 3, 2025

Overview

XtoMarkdown is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe that your documents are your business, and we have built our application to ensure that your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

XtoMarkdown does not collect any personal data.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any information about you or your documents. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reports sent to external servers
  • No network connections to external services
  • No telemetry of any kind

Local Processing

All document conversion happens entirely on your device. Your files are processed locally using the conversion engines (Pandoc and MarkItDown) bundled with the application. No file content is ever sent over the internet.

Local Storage

XtoMarkdown stores only your application preferences (such as your preferred conversion engine settings) locally on your device. This data never leaves your computer and is stored in the standard application data location for your operating system:

  • macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/xtomarkdown/
  • Windows: %LOCALAPPDATA%\xtomarkdown\
  • Linux: ~/.config/xtomarkdown/

Third-Party Services

XtoMarkdown does not integrate with any third-party services. The application works completely offline once installed.

Open Source

XtoMarkdown is open-source software licensed under the GPL-3.0 license. You can inspect the source code at any time to verify our privacy practices:

https://github.com/tx2z/xtomarkdown

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any data, XtoMarkdown does not collect any information from children under 13 years of age (or applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we do not collect any data, we do not have a way to notify users directly of changes.
